Skating Services

Our Skating Services

We offer a range of skating services to help you improve your skills and enjoy your time on the road:

  • Skating Lessons

    Our experienced instructors offer private and group lessons for skaters of all levels.
    Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine your technique, we can help you reach your goals.

  • Skate Rentals

    If you don't have your own skates, we offer rentals for both figure and hockey skates.
    Our rentals are available in a range of sizes, and our staff can help you find the perfect fit.

  • Skating Parties

    Looking for a fun and unique way to celebrate a birthday or special occasion?
    Our skating parties include private ice time, skate rentals, and refreshments for you and your guests.
